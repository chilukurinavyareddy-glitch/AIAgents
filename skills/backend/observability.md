# Skill: Backend Observability

Use when backend work needs logging, diagnostics, health, telemetry, or operational visibility.

## Guidance

- Add meaningful structured logs at important boundaries.
- Avoid logging secrets, tokens, passwords, or sensitive data.
- Include correlation identifiers when the project supports them.
- Ensure health/status endpoints are safe and do not leak internals.
- Prefer measurable events for important business or integration flows.

## Outputs

- Logging notes.
- Health/telemetry expectations.
- Operational validation notes.
