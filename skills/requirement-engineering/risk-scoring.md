# Skill: Requirement Risk Scoring

Use during clarification, requirement interpretation, architecture, and execution planning.

## Guidance

- Score risk based on ambiguity, security impact, data impact, integration impact, UX complexity, and testing complexity.
- Use risk score to decide whether clarification, architecture review, security review, or additional QA is needed.
- Do not inflate risk when a safe conservative assumption is enough.

## Outputs

- Risk score.
- Confidence level.
- Required guardrails.
